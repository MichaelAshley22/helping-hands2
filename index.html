What We Do

Helping Hands wants to be the one-stop shop for any, and all, of you pet care needs. From food & treats to emergency veterinary services, we have you covered! Our staff has a combined 50 years of experience and are equipped to handle all your pet-care needs. Our food selection has been carefully considered by our staff to ensure your four-legged family receives all the proper nutrition they require, at any stage of life. Our veterinary services include anything from vaccinations to minor surgeries and blood tests. We understand the desire to keep your pets safe and healthy throughout their life, which is why we aim to provide the highest level of pet-care we can possibly offer. While in our care, your pets will be treated as we would treat our own.

Among our staff is also a small team of trainers. We offer thorough obedience training and many simple commands. We understand the importance of having a well-trained dog, so we have put together our training program to add onto the plethora of our other services.

Who We Are

Helping Hands is a dedicated team of Vet Techs and Nutritionists on a mission to provide quality care for local pets. With our lead veterinarian at the head, our team of specialists will ensure your pets leave happy and healthy. We love to serve our community and strive to provide the same amount of care we would expect for pets of our own.
